The world while asleep


                                                                                                 There's

                                                                                                 a truth

                                                               not many know,        about

                                                                                                 the world          while you're asleep:

                                                                                                 That         the wind

                                                                                                 slips

                                                                                                 through        our window,

          

                                                                              To seal        the secrets        that you keep,

                                                                  And if                     you         don't believe me,

                                         Then you're welcome to your         doubt,

                                                     But have you never            

stopped

s        to wonder,

                                                                            What it's      whispering      about?

          

                                                                                                 What          else in the

                                                                              world,         Could         make the treetops bend and sway,

                                                                          But the           weight         of all

                                                                                                 the words,

                                                                                                 That         no one ever dared to say?

                                              And since it's while you             're          sleeping,

                                                                                                 Secrets         are easiest

                                                                                                 to        take

                                                                             It's               

no surprise

                                                                             those           with the deepest,

                                                                                                Are the ones

                                                                                                

kept wide awake

        

                - Erin Hanson
